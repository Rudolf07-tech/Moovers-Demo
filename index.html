The Problem
40%

Africa loses billions to broken cold chains.

Farmers, vendors, and pharmacies watch their livelihoods spoil in transit — not because of poor products, but because cold transport is inaccessible and unaffordable.

🌾

Farmers lose crops before market

Produce rots before reaching buyers, cutting income by up to 30%.

🐟

Fish & meat spoil in transit

Protein supply chains lack refrigeration, causing spoilage and safety risks.

💊

Medicines fail temperature requirements

Vaccines degrade without cold transport in last-mile delivery.

🚛

Cold trucks are expensive & unavailable

Industrial vehicles leave small traders with no viable option.

FAQ

Common questions.

Is Moovers available now?

⌄
We are in pilot development, building and testing our first units in Ghana. Sign up to be notified when we launch near you.

What makes Moovers different from regular delivery?

⌄
Moovers combines solar-powered active refrigeration, real-time temperature monitoring, GPS tracking, and AI-optimized logistics — purpose-built for Africa's informal economy.

Where will you operate first?

⌄
Ghana first — Accra, Kumasi, Techiman, and Cape Coast — with expansion across West Africa based on pilot results.

Can Moovers carry pharmaceutical products?

⌄
Yes. Our refrigeration supports 2°C–8°C required for vaccines and medicines, with full temperature logging for compliance.
