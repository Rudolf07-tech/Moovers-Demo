The Problem
40%

Africa loses billions to broken cold chains.

Farmers, vendors, and pharmacies watch their livelihoods spoil in transit — not because of poor products, but because cold transport is inaccessible and unaffordable.

🌾

Farmers lose crops before market

Produce harvested at peak freshness rots before reaching buyers, cutting farmer income by up to 30%.

🐟

Fish & meat spoil in transit

Protein supply chains lack refrigeration, causing spoilage, food safety risks, and price volatility.

💊

Medicines fail temperature requirements

Vaccines and temperature-sensitive drugs degrade without cold transport, especially in last-mile delivery.

🚛

Cold trucks are expensive & unavailable

Industrial refrigerated vehicles are designed for large volumes, leaving small traders with no viable option.

2

4

Delivered Fresh

FAQ

Common questions.

Is Moovers available now?

⌄
We are currently in the pilot development phase, building and testing our first refrigerated tricycle units in Ghana. Sign up for the waitlist to be notified when we launch in your area.

What makes Moovers different from regular delivery?

⌄
Moovers is the only delivery service in the region combining solar-powered active refrigeration (not passive ice), real-time temperature monitoring, GPS tracking, and an AI-optimized logistics platform — purpose-built for Africa's informal economy.

Where will you operate first?

⌄
Ghana — starting with urban and peri-urban markets in cities like Accra, Kumasi, Techiman, and Cape Coast, with expansion plans across West Africa based on pilot results.

How much does a delivery cost?

⌄
Pricing is designed to be affordable for SMEs and informal traders — significantly less than renting a refrigerated truck. Exact pricing will be published at launch. Join the waitlist for early-access pricing.

Can Moovers carry pharmaceutical products?

⌄
Yes. Our refrigeration systems support 2°C–8°C ranges required for most vaccines and temperature-sensitive medicines, with full temperature logging for regulatory compliance.
